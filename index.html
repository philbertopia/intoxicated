The days pass slowly, punctuated only by the occasional static crackle of the radio. You listen carefully, hoping for some sign of life beyond the endless forest and mountain range that surrounds you. The silence is unsettling, but you take solace in knowing that you are not alone. From time to time, you hear others reaching out, offering supplies or desperately seeking help. You suspect that there may be small pockets of survivors scattered throughout the region, but you've yet to encounter any of them.

The world you once knew has been destroyed by a cataclysmic meteor strike, leaving behind a wasteland of destruction, chaos, and terror. The impact has wiped out entire cities and decimated entire populations, plunging the world into darkness and despair. In the aftermath of the strike, a deadly pandemic has emerged, turning humans and animals alike into ravenous, flesh-eating monsters. You've witnessed their devastation firsthand and know that they can strike at any moment, lurking in the shadows and waiting for their next victim.

Despite the dangers that lurk outside, you've found a small measure of safety in the forest that surrounds you. It's a coniferous paradise, full of towering trees, crystal clear streams, and an abundance of wildlife. You've carved out a small existence for yourself, cultivating your own food and building a shelter that provides a modicum of protection against the elements and the horrors that roam beyond the treeline. The work is hard, and the days are long, but you take pride in knowing that you're able to survive in a world that has become increasingly hostile and unforgiving.

As the seasons pass, you find yourself growing restless, yearning for a sense of purpose beyond mere survival. You long to explore beyond the confines of the forest, to discover new communities, and to uncover the secrets that lie hidden in the ruins of the old world. But for now, you remain cautious, listening intently to the radio, and waiting for a sign that it's safe to venture forth once more.
